Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new collateral item?

Navigate to the Collateral page, click the "Add New" button, fill out the required fields, and click "Save".

How do I link collateral to a customer?

When adding or editing a collateral item, search for and select the customer name in the "Owner" field.

How do I generate a collateral report?

Go to the Reports page, select "Collateral Report" from the report type dropdown, set your filters, and click "Generate Report".
